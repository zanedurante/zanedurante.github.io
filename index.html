Bio

My name is Zane Durante, and I am currently a second-year PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Fei-Fei Li. My research interests are in multimodal visual understanding and applying artificial intelligence to improve healthcare. I am also broadly interested in creating AI systems that can reason about multiple input modalities and in applying AI for social good.
